Techknowledge Books

Techknowledge was founded by the inspiration of Late Shri. Pradeep Lunawat , who in the early 80’s established Pradeep Book Depot, which would distribute technical & non-technical academic books. Pradeep Book Depot was very soon trusted by academic communities and peers and thus he expanded into quality book distribution in collaboration with publishers.

After sometime, Shri. Pradeep realised that students, especially engineering aspirants were not able to find quality study materials. There was a huge gap between the prescribed syllabus and what was available in the local markets. To bridge this gap he established a publication house so eminent authors can publish quality content.
